Billing Model
Fixed (always-on 24/7): ML $61,797/mo + MC $3,256/mo + MP $690/mo = $65,743/mo baseline
Variable (per match): CDN GB delivery + ML $736/match + MC $19.26/match + MP anchor-interpolated cost
CDN costs: % Distribution mode distributes total GB across providers by %; Per-Provider GB mode takes actual per-provider GB from traffic reports
4K add-on: CH11_SPL only (+$8.244/hr). CH10_SPL is Reserved/Excluded
⚠ Scope: Media Elements (MediaConnect, MediaLive, MediaPackage — always AWS) plus the multi-provider CDN layer (CloudFront ME/EU, Wangsu, EdgeNext, Akamai). SCCC, Huawei, and Tencent are carried at an estimated $0.00839/GB. Model recalibrates at the start of each season.
Key Notes & Assumptions
TopicDetail
EDP Discount20% Enterprise Discount Program applied to all AWS rates — already baked into rate constants
Data scopeApril and May 2026 only — first two full months of SPL season on this infrastructure
MediaLive modelTwo selectable modes per match: Always-on $736/match (weighted average Apr $764 / May $703, current season default) or On-demand $17.028/hr rate card ($102.17 at 6h, for planning future seasons)
Per-match modelUses weighted historical averages — ML $736/match (±50%), MC $19.26/match (±25%). MP interpolates between billing anchors (2,650 / 55,790 / 378,210 viewers). Past or future matches use the same formula.
4K incremental4K adds CH11 only ($8.244/hr) — total 4K rate $25.272/hr. Minimum ~$87/match add-on
Subscriber pricingMonthly 70 SAR ($18.67/mo), Yearly 700 SAR ($15.56/mo avg). Rate: 3.75 SAR/USD
